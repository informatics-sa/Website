What are informatics olympiads?

Informatics olympiads is an competitive programming competition for students under the age of 20. The competition consists of analytical, mathematical, and logical problems, where the contestant is required to write code to solve the presented problems.

This e-sport relies on analytical, mathematical, and logical skills to deconstruct the problem and write its code efficiently and within a limited time.

Why informatics team?

The Saudi Informatics Team includes the strongest interests in competitive programming in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Team members are selected through a rigorous selection process that includes competitions and intensive training.

The team represents the Kingdom in the International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI) and the Regional Olympiad (APIO), and has achieved many accomplishments at the global level.

لماذا فريق المعلوماتية؟

يضم فريق المعلوماتية السعودي أقوى المهتمين في البرمجة التنافسية في المملكة العربية السعودية. يتم اختيار أعضاء الفريق من خلال عملية انتقاء صارمة تشمل مسابقات وتدريبات مكثفة.

يمثل الفريق المملكة في أولمبياد المعلوماتية الدولي (IOI) والإقليمي (APIO)، وقد حقق العديد من الإنجازات على المستوى العالمي.
